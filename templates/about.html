Welcome to 60 Seconds to Napoli

60 Seconds to Napoli brings the authentic taste of Naples to Germany and Switzerland. With over 30 locations, we've become the destination for award-winning Neapolitan pizza made with premium ingredients and traditional methods.

Our name comes from the exact 60 seconds it takes to cook a perfect Neapolitan pizza in our 485째C wood-fired oven. This high temperature creates a light, airy crust with just the right amount of char, while keeping the toppings fresh and flavorful.

Our dough rests for up to 72 hours, developing exceptional flavor and the perfect texture. We use only premium ingredients including San Marzano tomatoes DOP, Fior di Latte, and Parmigiano Reggiano 24 months aged.
